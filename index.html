Expert Nail Care & Maintenance Guide

With proper care, our Acrylic and Hard Gel extensions last between 3 to 5 weeks depending on your natural nail growth rate. We recommend booking a infill/refill session at the 3-week mark to maintain structural balance and keep your cuticles looking immaculate.
Absolutely! Abel specializes in recreating complex inspiration photos, customizing colors to match your skin undertone or outfit, and adding personal touches. Feel free to WhatsApp your reference images ahead of your appointment so we can prep specific charms or colors!
Please avoid applying heavy hand lotions, cuticle oils, or soaking your hands in water within 2 hours of your appointment. Clean, dry nails ensure maximum adhesion for your extensions. If you have existing gel or acrylic from another salon, please let us know when booking so we allocate gentle removal time.
